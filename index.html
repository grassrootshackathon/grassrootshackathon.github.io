Cannabis + Technology

GrassRoots was created because there are still a lot of problems left unsolved in the newly-legalized cannabis industry.

Our goal is to create an event where those interested in the tech-side of this industry can find each other and build something amazing together.

We're bringing together designers, developers, and entrepreneurs who share a passion for cannabis and a desire to experience what it takes to launch a cannabis technology startup!





When: May 29-31

Where: WeWork Seattle (500 Yale Avenue North, Seattle WA 98109)

Hours: Friday 6pm - 10pm
Saturday 9am - 10pm
Sunday 9am - 9pm

 

Who Should Attend?

GrassRoots is for anyone excited about the intersection of cannabis and technology. You don't need to be a veteran programmer or experienced entrepreneur to attend - you just need to be passionate about cannabis and willing to spend a weekend building and launching a company.
